Hello!

I am a Mexican American digital media artist currently studying at San Jose State University.

Art is supposed to evoke a reaction from someone whether it be physical or emotional, therefore that is what I aim to do with my art. Through pastel colors and a playful art style my work tries to evoke an eye-catching physical response from the audience while also targeting serious topics that would catch the emotions of the viewer. I mainly focus on mental health, Mexican culture, queer representation, and fanart in my compositions. My art mainly consist of digital creations such as animations, animatics, digital art, and video games. However, I do dabble with studio art and once in a while will produce paintings and physical drawings.

Digital art shapes my work because it was the first thing that inspired me to go down the path of being an artist. However, physical art helped me improve significantly as an artist. I hope viewers are able to see that even with a cute simplistic style you can be considered a serious art who tackles important topics.
